Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about choosing roofing companies in Galway

How much does roofing cost in Galway?

Roofing costs in Galway vary depending on roof type, materials, and project size. Residential projects typically range from €5,000-€15,000, with factors like roof complexity and material choice affecting costs. Commercial projects often start at €10,000. Request multiple quotes for accurate pricing.

How long does a roof replacement take in Galway?

Residential roof replacements in Galway typically take 3-7 days, depending on weather, roof size, and complexity. Commercial projects may take 2-4 weeks. Galway’s wet climate can cause delays, so plan for extra time during rainy seasons.

What roofing materials work best in Galway's climate?

Slate, clay tiles, and modern materials like EPDM and GRP fibreglass are ideal for Galway’s wet and windy climate. These materials withstand frequent rain and strong coastal winds. Consult your contractor for the best options based on your property and budget.

Do I need planning permission for roof work in Galway?

Most roof repairs and like-for-like replacements in Galway don’t require planning permission under exempted development rules. However, significant alterations or work on protected structures may need approval from Galway City or County Council. Check with your contractor for specific requirements.

How do I find the best roofers near me in Galway?
