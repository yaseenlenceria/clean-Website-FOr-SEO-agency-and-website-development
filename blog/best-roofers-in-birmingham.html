  • Insurance and licensing: Ensure they're properly insured and licensed
  • Written quotes: Get detailed written estimates from multiple contractors
  • Warranty: Look for companies offering comprehensive warranties
  • Experience: Choose contractors with proven experience in your type of roofing project
