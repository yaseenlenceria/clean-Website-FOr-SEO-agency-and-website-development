Frequently Asked Questions

How much do dental marketing agencies charge?

Most dental marketing agencies charge £500-£3000 per month depending on services. Basic SEO packages start around £500/mo, while comprehensive solutions including PPC, content, and social media typically range £1500-£3000/mo.

What should I look for in a dental marketing agency?

Look for: 1) Dental industry experience 2) Portfolio of successful clients 3) Transparent reporting 4) Understanding of GDC compliance 5) Comprehensive services (SEO, PPC, website design) 6) Clear communication processes.

How long does it take to see results from dental marketing?

SEO results typically appear in 3-6 months, while PPC can generate immediate leads. A complete marketing strategy usually shows significant practice growth within 6-12 months of consistent implementation.

Should I hire a general marketing agency or dental specialist?

Specialized dental marketers understand the unique aspects of patient acquisition in dentistry, GDC compliance requirements, and how to effectively market different treatments. This specialized knowledge typically delivers better results.

What's more important for dental practices - SEO or PPC?

Both are important. SEO builds long-term, sustainable visibility while PPC provides immediate results. Most successful practices use a combination, with PPC supporting during the 3-6 month period it takes SEO to gain traction.
