The Digital Real Estate Landscape in 2025

The real estate market has fundamentally changed. Today's property buyers and sellers are more informed, tech-savvy, and demanding than ever before. Understanding this new landscape is crucial for success.

Key Statistics Shaping Real Estate Marketing

  • 97% of buyers start their property search online
  • 89% of sellers research agents online before making contact
  • 73% of buyers watch video content during their property search
  • 68% of millennials use their mobile device as the primary tool for house hunting
  • 84% of buyers want virtual tours before visiting properties
  • 92% of buyers read online reviews before choosing an agent

The Modern Property Buyer Journey

Understanding how buyers move through their journey helps you create targeted marketing:

  1. Awareness Stage: Researching areas, prices, and market conditions
  2. Consideration Stage: Comparing properties and shortlisting options
  3. Decision Stage: Choosing specific properties and agents
  4. Post-Purchase: Sharing experiences and becoming advocates

Digital Channels That Matter Most

  • Search Engines (SEO/PPC): 76% of property searches start here
  • Social Media: 64% of buyers use social platforms for research
  • Property Portals: 82% of buyers visit Rightmove or Zoopla
  • Estate Agent Websites: 58% visit agent sites directly
  • Email Marketing: 41% want regular property updates
  • Video Platforms: 37% watch property videos on YouTube
