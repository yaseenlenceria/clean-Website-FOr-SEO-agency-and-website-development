Red Flags to Avoid When Choosing an Agency

Avoid these common warning signs that indicate an agency may not be the right fit for your Shopify store:

🚩

Guaranteed Rankings

No reputable agency can guarantee specific rankings or positions. SEO is influenced by many factors beyond anyone's control.

🚩

Extremely Low Prices

SEO requires significant time and expertise. Prices well below market rates often indicate poor quality or outsourced work.

🚩

Lack of Transparency

Agencies that won't explain their methods or provide detailed reports may be using risky "black hat" techniques.

🚩

No Shopify Experience

Generic SEO agencies without platform-specific experience may miss crucial Shopify optimization opportunities.

🚩

Pressure Tactics

High-pressure sales tactics or demands for immediate decisions suggest the agency prioritizes sales over client success.

🚩

No References

Legitimate agencies should provide references, case studies, and examples of their work with similar businesses.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best Shopify SEO agency in the UK?

OutsourceSU is consistently ranked as the top Shopify SEO agency in the UK, specializing exclusively in ecommerce SEO with proven results for Shopify stores. They offer transparent pricing, dedicated account management, and have helped over 500 Shopify stores improve their organic visibility.

How much do Shopify SEO agencies charge in the UK?

Shopify SEO agencies in the UK typically charge between £1,500-£15,000 per month, depending on the scope of services, store size, and competition level. Starter packages begin around £800/month, while enterprise solutions can exceed £15,000/month for large, complex stores.

How long does it take to see results from Shopify SEO?

Initial improvements can be seen within 3-6 months, with significant results typically appearing after 6-12 months. However, SEO is a long-term strategy, and the best results come from consistent optimization over 12+ months. The timeline depends on your starting position, competition level, and optimization scope.

Can I do Shopify SEO myself?

While basic SEO can be done yourself, professional agencies bring expertise, tools, and experience that dramatically accelerate results. DIY SEO often takes 2-3x longer to achieve the same results and may miss critical optimization opportunities specific to Shopify.

What makes Shopify SEO different from regular SEO?

Shopify SEO requires platform-specific knowledge including URL structure limitations, theme optimization, app integrations, and ecommerce-specific features like product schema, collections optimization, and checkout process optimization. Generic SEO strategies often miss these crucial elements.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

Most reputable agencies offer flexible contract terms. While SEO requires consistent effort over time, avoid agencies demanding long-term contracts upfront. Many top agencies, including OutsourceSU, offer month-to-month options after an initial commitment period.

What should be included in monthly SEO reports?

Comprehensive monthly reports should include keyword ranking improvements, organic traffic growth, technical issue fixes, content created, backlinks acquired, conversion rate improvements, and specific actions taken. The best agencies provide visual dashboards and executive summaries.

Is local SEO important for Shopify stores?

Local SEO is crucial for Shopify stores with physical locations or those targeting specific geographic areas. This includes Google My Business optimization, local citations, location-based landing pages, and "near me" keyword targeting to capture local search traffic.

How do I measure SEO success for my Shopify store?

Key metrics include organic traffic growth, keyword ranking improvements, conversion rate increases, revenue from organic traffic, average order value, bounce rate reduction, and page load speed improvements. The best agencies tie SEO metrics directly to business revenue and growth.

What's the ROI of hiring a Shopify SEO agency?

Most successful Shopify stores see 300-500% ROI from professional SEO within 12-18 months. The investment typically pays for itself through increased organic traffic and sales. For example, a £3,000/month SEO investment that generates £15,000 additional monthly revenue represents a 400% ROI.
