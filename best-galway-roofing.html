Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about choosing roofing companies in Galway

How much does roofing cost in Galway?

Roofing costs in Galway vary depending on the type of roof, materials, and size. Typical costs range from €5,000-€15,000 for residential properties, with factors like roof complexity, material choice, and accessibility affecting the final price. Commercial projects typically start from €10,000. Always get multiple quotes for accurate pricing specific to your project.

How long does a roof replacement take in Galway?

Most residential roof replacements take 3-7 days, depending on weather conditions, roof size, and complexity. Larger commercial projects may take 2-4 weeks. Galway's wet climate can cause delays, so allow extra time during winter months. Your contractor should provide a realistic timeline based on your specific project requirements.

What roofing materials work best in Galway's climate?

Slate, clay tiles, and modern materials like EPDM rubber and GRP fibreglass are ideal for Galway's wet and windy climate. These materials withstand frequent rain and strong coastal winds effectively. Your contractor can recommend the best option based on your building type, budget, and aesthetic preferences.

Do I need planning permission for roof work in Galway?

Most roof repairs and like-for-like replacements don't require planning permission under permitted development rights. However, significant changes, extensions, or work on protected structures may need approval from Galway City or County Council. Your roofing contractor should advise on any permissions needed.

How do I find the best roofers near me in Galway?

Look for local Galway roofers with excellent customer reviews, proper insurance, and relevant certifications. Check their portfolio of recent work, ask for references, and get detailed written quotes. Our top 10 list includes vetted contractors across Galway, all with proven track records of quality work.

What should I look for in Galway roofing contractors?

Essential factors include: valid public liability insurance, proper licenses and certifications, local reputation and references, detailed written quotes, clear warranty terms, and membership in trade associations.
