Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about contractor SEO and digital marketing

How long does it take to see SEO results for contractors?

Most contractors see initial improvements in 3-6 months, with significant results typically achieved within 6-12 months. Local SEO improvements can be seen faster, often within 1-3 months.

What makes contractor SEO different from other industries?

Contractor SEO focuses heavily on local search, project-based keywords, visual content showcasing work, and trust signals like reviews and certifications that are crucial in the construction industry.

How much should contractors invest in SEO monthly?

Most successful contractors invest £1,000-£5,000 monthly in SEO, depending on their market size and competition. The ROI typically ranges from 300-500% within the first year.

Can small contractors compete with larger companies online?

Absolutely! Small contractors often have advantages in local SEO and can target specific niches. With the right strategy, they can outrank larger competitors in their service areas.
